Please refer to the troubleshooting guide below:


Is the "Caps Lock" enabled on your keyboard?
The username and password must be lowercase. Please confirm that "Caps Lock" is disabled on your keyboard and Reload.

Forgot your username or password?
Please reset the device to factory default settings if you have forgotten your username and password.
Note: The settings will be restored to factory default settings once the device is reset.

How to restore the device to the factory default settings?
While the device is powered on, locate the reset button on the rear panel of the unit and hold the reset button for about 8-10 seconds. The device will reboot and restore itself to the factory default settings. Reset button for more than 5 seconds, the device will then reboot and restore itself to the factory default settings.
